     Your quest to find the perfect career will be more successful if 
you try to maximize the opportunities that are available. For example, 
you might consider working with an organization that offers you 
career counseling. A career counselor is trained to help you determine 
your aptitudes and interests. You may contact people who work in a 
career that interests you and ask to shadow them on their jobs and ask 
them questions. You might find an online service to help you find a 
very interesting career that will meet each of your goals.
